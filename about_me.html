About Me

Hi there! My name is Raiza Torres Acosta, and I am a 21-year-old multimedia artist based in Miami, Florida. I take from my cultures that value a lot of color and movement in their legacies, Mexican and Cuban, and which inspire me to see the world in my own special light. I also take a lot of inspirations from pop culture such as ani-manga and indie projects online as the internet was a very important part of my life and growing up.
